About

MLB, or Major League Baseball, orginizes its teams into two arbitrary leagues with three roughly geographical divisions each. National League Central, or NL Central, is a division geographically spanning across much of the Midwest and Rustbelt. Most famous for its historical Cardinals-Cubs rivalry, it is one of the most compeditive divisons in baseball.

Teams

NL Central is home to the following 5 teams:

  • St. Louis Cardinals
  • Chicago Cubs
  • Miliwakee Brewers
  • Cincinatti Reds
  • Pittsburg Pirates
