The History of NL Central

According to baseball-reference.com, NL Central was created in 1994 following a reallignment of the MLB into 6 divisions. Orignially containing the Cubs, Reds, Astros, Pirates, and Cardinals, NL Central has realligned twice (once in 1998, once in 2013) to contain the teams we see in it today.
